mod.ts
The mod.ts file is usually the entry point of your module. The Node.js equivalent would be index.js. Here we expose everything 
of our public API. Depending on how large your project is, you wanna keep the mod.ts file clean by only using import and 
export-statements, rather than defining actual functionality.
